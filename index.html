Clay Evans

I am an aspiring game developer looking to make an impact in the game industry. I graduated from California State University, Monterey Bay in December 2017 with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, concentrating in Game Development. My major areas of expertise are game development and graphics programming. During my college experience, I worked as a graphics researcher with the Game Research Lab as well as a TA for a graphics class and a VR game jam. I am also interested in game engine development and upcoming technologies that allow players to interact more with games, such as VR, AR, and physical add-ons (e.g. Nintendo Labo, Virtuix Omni).

When I am not coding, I love to travel, hike, and eat cheese. I also love to game, my favorite titles of the moment being Super Mario Odyssey, Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, and Grand Theft Auto V.

You can contact me at evansclay [at] gmail [dot] com.
